From consult to glow plan.

A simple process reduces uncertainty and helps clients feel guided before they book.

Book consult

2

Skin review

The provider reviews goals, current routine, skin history, and suitable treatment options.

3

Treatment plan

The client receives a realistic plan based on safety, budget, skin needs, and desired outcome.

4

Aftercare

The client gets aftercare guidance, follow-up timing, and maintenance recommendations.
