Simple ordering flow.

A good food store website does not need to be complicated. It just needs to make buying easier.

2

3

4

Questions customers ask before buying.

FAQs reduce repeated calls and make the business look more organized.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes, local delivery can be listed here if the store offers it. Delivery area and fees can also be added.

Can customers call before coming?

Yes. A phone or WhatsApp button can help customers ask what is available before visiting.

What products do you sell?

The website can list groceries, produce, snacks, drinks, pantry items, frozen foods, and other store items.

Can this website be updated later?

Yes. Product categories, phone numbers, photos, hours, and delivery information can be updated as needed.
