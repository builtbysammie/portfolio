The Process

Vision to
reality.

A clear, personal process from first conversation to final setup — so you always know where your event stands and what's coming next.

01

Consultation

We discuss your event date, venue, guest count, style direction, and budget. No pressure — just a conversation to understand your vision.

02

Concept Direction

We shape the mood, palette, key decor moments, and realistic scope. You receive a clear design direction before committing.

03

Planning & Preparation

Florals, rentals, layout, vendor coordination, and final styling details — all confirmed and prepared ahead of your date.
