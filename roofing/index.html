Emergency-ready

Roofing pages need urgency and trust.

Many roofing customers are not casually browsing. They may have a leak, visible damage, or stress after a storm. The website needs to reduce panic and move them toward action.

Clear estimate button The primary action is visible from the hero section and repeated near the bottom.

Process

Simple repair process.

A process section helps homeowners understand what happens after they request an estimate.

1

Request inspection

The homeowner sends their address, issue, photos if available, and preferred inspection time.

2

Check the roof

The team reviews the damage, finds the cause, and explains what needs to be repaired.

3

Get clear estimate

The homeowner receives a simple explanation, price range, timeline, and recommended next step.

4

Complete the work

The repair or replacement is scheduled, completed, cleaned up, and reviewed with the homeowner.
