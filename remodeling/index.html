Process

From idea to finished space.

A strong remodeling site should reduce uncertainty by explaining what happens before, during, and after the project.

1

Consultation

The homeowner shares the project idea, budget range, timeline, and what needs to change.

2

Planning

The team reviews layout, materials, design direction, scope, and realistic project expectations.

3

Build phase

The remodel is scheduled, coordinated, communicated, and completed with progress updates.

4

Final walkthrough

The finished work is reviewed, details are checked, and the homeowner understands care steps.
