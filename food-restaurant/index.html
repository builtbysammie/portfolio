Order Flow

Restaurant websites should remove friction.

How It Works

Simple customer journey.

This kind of page is built so customers can go from hunger to order without thinking too hard.

1

Choose meal

The customer scans the menu, bestsellers, prices, and available meal options.

2

Order or reserve

They call, message, reserve a table, or use the order button depending on the business setup.

3

Pickup, delivery, or dine-in

The customer chooses pickup, delivery, or dining in and confirms timing or location details.

4

Enjoy and return

The restaurant delivers a smooth experience and encourages repeat orders or future visits.
